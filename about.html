Who We Are

About VibeChat

A product built with passion, Flutter, and real-time Firebase magic.

VibeChat was born from a simple idea: messaging apps should feel alive. We wanted to build something that looks great, works instantly, and is a joy to use every single day. So we picked Flutter for its beautiful, native-quality UI and Firebase for its lightning-fast real-time backend — and VibeChat was born.

Our Mission

To make human connection effortless. We believe that technology should bring people closer, not add friction. VibeChat strips away the clutter and focuses on what matters — having genuine, real-time conversations with the people you care about.

The Technology

VibeChat is built entirely using Flutter, Google's open-source UI toolkit, allowing us to ship a beautiful, high-performance Android app. Our backend leverages Firebase Realtime Database for instant message delivery, Firebase Authentication for secure login (including Google Sign-In), and Firebase Cloud Storage for media hosting.
